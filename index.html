Team

This website is the result of 8 weeks of an intense website development bootcamp given by the Turing Society at the University of Amsterdam. During these weeks we learned the basics of HTML, CSS, jQuery, APIs and frameworks such as Bootstrap. So please! Have a look around, learn cool new facts about some of the best specialty beers in the World, generate random beer jokes and get to know our team.
